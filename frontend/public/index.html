How it works

Connect

Link your Spotify in one click. Just listen to music as usual. Emotify does the rest.

Analyze

Each track gets mapped to a mood: Joy, Anger, Sadness, or Pleasure based on its acoustic character.

Discover

See how your listening shifts day to day. Spot patterns. Understand yourself a little better.

Four emotional moods

High Energy
Low Energy
Positive

Joy

Upbeat, energetic, happy music

Pleasure

Calm, relaxing, content music

Negative

Anger

Intense, aggressive, powerful music

Sadness

Melancholic, slow, reflective music

These moods describe the music itself — not a diagnosis of how you feel.

Music is personal. If a prediction feels off, you can always correct it — and the system learns your taste over time.

Your data, your control

We never sell your data.

Spotify data is used only as behavioral context filters, not for ML training (per Spotify API terms).

You can delete your account and all data at any time.
