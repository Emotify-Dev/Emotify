## Scale

Both models output valence and arousal on the **DEAM scale (1–9)**, where 5 is the midpoint.

| Quadrant | Valence | Arousal |
|----------|---------|---------|
| Joy | ≥ 5 | ≥ 5 |
| Anger | < 5 | ≥ 5 |
| Pleasure | ≥ 5 | < 5 |
| Sadness | < 5 | < 5 |
